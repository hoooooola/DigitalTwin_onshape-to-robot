## 🚀 專案願景 (Vision)
歡迎來到 **Project Hornet**！我們的目標是打造一個高精確度的「數位孿生」(Digital Twin) 開發平台。
我們不僅是在電腦裡模擬，更是要建立一個從 CAD 設計、精確 STL 輸出到模擬環境，並最終與實機進行雙向驗證的完整閉環系統。

如果你對無人機、飛行控制、機器學習或機器人仿真感興趣，這裡是你的最佳遊樂場！

Welcome to **Project Hornet**! Our goal is to build a high-precision "Digital Twin" development platform.
We are not just simulating in a computer, but building a complete closed-loop system from CAD design, precise STL export to simulation environments, and ultimately two-way validation with the real drone.

If you are interested in drones, flight control, machine learning, or robotics simulation, this is your best playground!

## 🎯 核心里程碑 (Core Milestones)
*   **[Pegasus 整合]**: 將 Hornet 的精密模型導入 Pegasus Simulator 中，深度整合 Isaac Sim。
*   **[多仿真鏈路]**: 支援 Gazebo 仿真，並利用 PX4 與 ROS 2 進行先進飛控開發。
*   **[AI 強化學習]**: 在虛擬空間進行大規模自動飛行控制與 AI 避障/控制訓練。
*   **[虛實雙向驗證]**: 透過 STL 零件 3D 列印實機，將仿真數據與飛行數據進行比對校準。

*   **[Pegasus Integration]**: Import the precise Hornet model into Pegasus Simulator, deeply integrating with Isaac Sim.
*   **[Multi-Simulation Links]**: Support Gazebo simulation, and utilize PX4 and ROS 2 for advanced flight control development.
*   **[AI Reinforcement Learning]**: Conduct large-scale automated flight control and AI obstacle avoidance/control training in virtual space.
*   **[Virtual-Real Validation]**: Use 3D printed actual parts from STLs to compare and calibrate simulation data with real flight data.
